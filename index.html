About Porsche

Founded in 1931 by Ferdinand Porsche in Stuttgart, Porsche has stood for innovation, precision and timeless design from day one. What began as an engineering office became one of the most iconic sports car brands.

The Porsche philosophy is to blend performance with everyday usability. From the legendary 911 to modern electric models like the Macan and Taycan, every Porsche is engineered to deliver pure driving emotion.

More than cars, Porsche represents dreams realized through engineering excellence and a relentless pursuit of perfection.
